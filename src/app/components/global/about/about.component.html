TerraHub: Explore Terraria easier!

Meet TerraHub, a fan-made web application that exploits useful information about the Terraria video game and serves as a practical tool to use while playing our favourite video game.

TerraHub gathers all its data from the official Terraria Wiki website. It aims to bring all the Terraria data together in one convenient place, making it a practical tool for your gaming adventures. Among its best features, TerraHub allows you to easily access lists of all items and NPCs in the game, while having the flexibility to search and filter them by various categories. It's a handy way to find what you need quickly.

It's important to note that TerraHub is not affiliated with the creators of Terraria. Instead, it's a project made with love by fans. All the in-game content used on TerraHub belongs to Re-Logic, the creators of Terraria..
